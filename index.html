CVID-19 Situational Risk Calculator

This is a tool to help you think more clearly about whether or not to attend an in-person event.

This is not medical advice! If you are unclear on your risk, or have a pre-existing condition that makes you especially vulnerable to COVID-19, talk to your doctor.
